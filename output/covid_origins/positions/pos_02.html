This position fundamentally opposes the consensus that the COVID-19 pandemic originated from natural zoonosis, instead arguing that the lab leak hypothesis is the most credible explanation based on available evidence and analysis.

This position emphasizes that credible early infection evidence aligns with the lab leak hypothesis and that key assumptions used to dismiss it are flawed. It challenges the reliability of zoonotic explanations, highlighting potential biases and uncertainties in natural origin theories, and underscores the plausibility of a lab-related origin based on the timing, location, and scientific analyses.

Core commitment: The pandemic most likely resulted from a laboratory leak rather than natural animal-to-human transmission.
