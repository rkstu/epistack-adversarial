This position uniquely argues that eggs are a valuable, nutrient-rich food that can be included in a diet despite their cholesterol content, emphasizing their affordability and nutritional benefits.

This position highlights that eggs are an affordable and nutrient-dense food, rich in high-quality protein and micronutrients, and distinguishes itself by asserting that their cholesterol content does not outweigh their nutritional benefits. It challenges the notion that dietary cholesterol from eggs is inherently problematic, emphasizing their role as a healthy food choice.

Core commitment: Eggs are a nutritious, affordable food source that provides essential nutrients with minimal saturated fats, and their cholesterol content should not be a reason to avoid them.
