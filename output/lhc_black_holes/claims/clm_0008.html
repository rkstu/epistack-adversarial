Source & Provenance

"The LHC reproduces in the laboratory, under controlled conditions, collisions at centre-of-mass energies less than those reached in the atmosphere by some of the cosmic rays that have been bombarding the Earth for billions of years."

Source: Study of potentially dangerous events during heavy-ion collisions at the LHC · Original

Belongs To

This position uniquely argues that collisions at the LHC are inherently safe because they are comparable to or less energetic than natural cosmic ray events that have been occurring for billions of years without catastrophic consequences.
